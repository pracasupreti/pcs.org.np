About Praudh Computer Shiksha

We're for social causes

We are working with the dream towards the 100 % digital literacy since 2010 A.D.

Praudh Computer Shikshya is the program brought by Prakash Infosys in fulfillment of its social responsibility, Praudh Computer Shikshya is purely service oriented and will be conducted free of cost. In this program, people of any profession, religion, community, etc. can participate and become competent by getting information about email, internet, Facebook, Skype, chat, and basic computer skills.We now have different programs for different people such as praudh computer shiksha for over 30 years of age, Express Computing for government and organization's officer's, Woman Digital literacy for woman, e-bidding for the contractors and Digital Marketing as well to the people who know the basic of the computer.

Praudh Computer Shikshya is a completely free service. You do not have to pay any money after the training period. We have not received any money from anywhere to run this program. We just want all Nepalis to have access to email, internet. The Internet may have reached every village and town market, but few people know how to use it. We want to reduce the complete technology gap between the people.

Starting from Biratnagar, this program will be found in the northern part of Morang, Letang, other parts of Morad district, Koshi zone and across the country. You can talk to your relatives whenever you want by being familiar with email, internet etc.You can boost your working effiecient. Time is changing, so don't miss the opportunity to access the Internet and the computer in time.

Fulfill our dream

Let's make a change with Digital Literacy

Enhance the Digital Literacy

World is growing rapidly with technology but there are still people who have never been familiar to the technology, we aim to make them technology friendly. We Plan to reach every remote area of the country by 2025.

Increase the Woman Digital Literacy rate

As our country's woman have been always been under shadow, we want to literate them digitally, to use the basic technology used in day to day life and the software to shape their life. Woman will be able to work in the computer.

Enhance Government Officer

Recently technology have replaced all the paper work in the government sector and most of the government officer are not technology friendly, we train them to work smoothly and comfortably with the technology introduced.

Train People For Digital Marketing

Along with digital illiteracy unemployment is also major problem,we train people for digital marketing from which they can even grab the different jobs in digital marketing as the digital marketing have been the necessity for today's world.

E-bidding

As the technology have been taking over all the paperwork and other things, bidding is also have become online, and we always insist people to be technology friendly, so we train people specially to the contractors who want to bid online.

Accounting from computer

We aim to have the population to atleast use the accounting software like Tally and excel to those who have basic knowledge of the computer, so we plan to reach most of the remote areas and teach those software so that they can be hired easily.
