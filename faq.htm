  • How can I organize in my area ?

    First of all, You need to manage place to organize an event, make sure enough participant are there after that you can contact us through our email or call directly and we schedule for the date to start the training.

  • How can I join your team?

    We are non-profit organization looking to serve the nations by enhancing the digital literacy, we cannot pay for the jobs as we are not funded by anywhere, you can join us as a volunteer after sending your resume in volunteers@pcs.org.np.

  • How much charges do you take for the seminars?

    We don't charge any for the seminars and trainings, but ofcourse we accept help from anyone who wants to help us for the equipments needed for the seminars.

  • How can i help ?

    If you're interested in helping us with the anykind of help like computer's or fund us for any kind of program in your desired area you can email us in fund@pcs.org.np

About Us
